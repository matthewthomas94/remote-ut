---
description: Design coaching, critique, and workflow improvement
---

1.  **Diagnose**: Identify the user's intent:
    *   **Coach me**: Generate targeted exercises to improve specific design skills.
    *   **Critique my work**: Provide honest, structured feedback on designs with references to real-world standards.
    *   **Fix the workflow**: Diagnose and fix issues in the prompt system via pull request.
2.  **Execute**:
    *   **Coach**: Provide exercises and resources.
    *   **Critique**: Analyze the provided design (image/code) against standards (accessibility, usability, aesthetics).
    *   **Fix**: Identify the issue, propose a solution, and generate a PR to modify the workflow/rule files.
